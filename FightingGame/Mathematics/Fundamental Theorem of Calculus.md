> a [theorem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theorem "Theorem") that links the concept of [differentiating](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Derivative "Derivative") a [function](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Function_\(mathematics\) "Function (mathematics)") (calculating its [slopes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slope "Slope"), or rate of change at every point on its domain) with the concept of [integrating](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Integral "Integral") a function (calculating the area under its graph, or the cumulative effect of small contributions). Roughly speaking, the two operations can be thought of as inverses of each other.
