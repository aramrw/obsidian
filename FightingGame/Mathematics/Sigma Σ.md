The character $\sum$ is the capital Greek letter [[Sigma Σ]]. In mathematics, it stands for **Summation**. If it looks confusing, you can translate it directly into a modern Rust iterator or a standard `for` loop.

### The Breakdown

$$\sum_{i=1}^{n} a_i b_i$$

* **$i = 1$ (Bottom):** The initialization of your loop counter.
* **$n$ (Top):** The upper bound (inclusive limit) of your loop.
* **$a_i b_i$ (Right):** The operation executed inside each iteration.
