> A **gyroid** is an infinitely connected [triply periodic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Triply_periodic_minimal_surface "Triply periodic minimal surface") [minimal surface](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minimal_surface "Minimal surface") discovered by [Alan Schoen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alan_Schoen "Alan Schoen") in 1970.[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gyroid#cite_note-1)[[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gyroid#cite_note-hoffman-2) It arises naturally in polymer science and biology, as an interface with high surface area.
