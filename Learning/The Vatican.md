 #異端血統
## ヴァチカン
![[Pasted image 20260504192519.png|171]]

This is not a congregation of peaceful priests. The Vatican presence in Hell is a hyper-violent, fanatical military expedition. 

To them, demons, the damned, and especially magic-users are filth to be purged with extreme, psychotic prejudice. They do not save souls; they exterminate them with consecrated lead.

## The Relationship with the Player (The Betrayal)

**Rings 1-5 (The Convenient Tool):** When the player drops into Limbo, the Vatican army leaders view you as a useful ally. As you progress, they ask you to join The special [[ヴァチカン特務第13 課イスカリオテ機関]]. 

**Ring 6 (The Excommunication):** The moment the player interacts with [[Darzog]] and awakens their **Heretical Bloodline**, the alliance shatters instantly. There is no trial, no dialogue of redemption. The Vatican’s tolerance drops to absolute zero. You transition from a useful dog to a priority target. The hunters become the hunted, and the full, terrifying might of the Inquisition is turned entirely on you.

## Key Figures

### [[Shotgun Monk (Final Boss)]] (The Zealot)

- **The Archetype:** He wields a massive, customized trench shotgun loaded with silver-flake buckshot and blessed incendiary shells. 
    
- **Progression:** He starts as your calm squad leader in the upper rings. After the Schism, he becomes your relentless pursuer for a final showdown in **Ring 9: [[Treachery]]**.

### Motive for Hunting Satan:
* The Vatican wants to seize control of hell and rule over it. See: [[The Orthodox Church (東方正教会)]]
* They want to turn hell into a private prison and utilize the condemned as slaves.
