### Pope Saint Leo IX

#異端血統

![[Pasted image 20260504190748.png|154]]
- 
  The Shotgun Monk is a recurring antagonist part of the  [[The Vatican]]'s "[[ヴァチカン特務第13 課イスカリオテ機関 ]]" who evolves throughout the game. By the time the player reaches the final ring, he reveals himself as **Pope Saint Leo IX** (1049-1054)--the final boss of the game. His ultimate goal is to break [[Satan]] out of his cage and destroy him.
  
- **The Final Confrontation:** He serves as the final showdown for the player in **Ring 9: [[Treachery]]**. While the player is navigating the frozen, crystalline wasteland to reach [[Satan]], the Pope acts as the primary obstacle, representing [[The Vatican]]'s militant opposition to the player's [[Heretical Bloodline (異端血統)]].
  
* **Thematic Conflict:** His presence highlights the tension between the player and [[The Vatican]]. Even though both parties are heading toward [[Satan]], their motivations are fundamentally different, with the Shotgun Monk serving as the enforcer of the Vatican's dogma against the player's "heretical" nature.
