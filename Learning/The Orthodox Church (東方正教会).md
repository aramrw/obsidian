## The Core Philosophy (The Warden's Duty)

The Orthodox Church, founded by [[Darzog]] in Ring 6: [[地獄の六輪：Heresy]], operates on one dogma: **Hell is not a realm to be conquered; it is meant to be contained.** 

To the Orthodox, the universe requires balance. The 9th Ring: [[Treachery]] is a frozen prison. The Orthodox doctrine dictates that the duty of the enlightened is not to eradicate Hell, but to become its eternal Wardens. They embrace the dark, heretical magic of the abyss because it is the only tool strong enough to maintain the prison's bars.
## The Heresy of the  The Vatican

From the Orthodox perspective, [[The Vatican]]'s XIIIth Crusade is an apocalyptic display of blinding hubris.

The Orthodox Church understands the Vatican's true, terrifying motive: The Catholic forces intend to shatter the [[Ice Prison]] of Ring 9, unfreeze the Devil, and permanently destroy him. While this sounds like a righteous crusade, Darzog and the player realize the catastrophic reality. Satan is the load-bearing pillar of Hell's ecosystem. If the Vatican successfully kills him, the cosmic balance fractures. Hell's infrastructure will collapse, allowing the psychotic, power-hungry Vatican forces to absorb the Underworld entirely and establish a tyrannical, unchecked theological empire over both the living and the dead.

## The Player's Ascent (The First Patriarch)

When the player formally breaks from [[The Vatican]] and founds the Orthodox Church, their role shifts dramatically.

- **From Mercenary to Warden:** You are no longer just fighting to survive; you are fighting to defend the deepest rings of Hell from the Crusaders. You are actively trying to stop the Vatican from reaching the bottom.
    
- **The Tools of the Trade:** The Church merges the ancient, arcane Byzantine magic of 1054.
    
- **The Burden:** The Orthodox Church does not promise salvation or a return to the surface. It promises a permanent, bloody shift defending the [[Ice Prison]] of Ring 9.
