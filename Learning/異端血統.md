# The 9 Circles (World Structure)

**Tags:**  #異端血統
## The Upper Hell (Rings 1-5)

The early game is desolate, confusing, and focused purely on survival.

- **Ring 1: [[Limbo]]**
    
    - The player drops in with zero cinematic introduction. Just a gray, ash-covered wasteland.
        
    - Populated by "Virtuous Pagans" who weren't evil, but existed outside the Church's light.
        
    - **Key Encounter:** [[The Buddha]] resides here, peacefully observing the theological war tearing Hell apart.
        
- **Rings 2-5: Lust, Gluttony, Greed, Wrath**
    
    - Navigating these requires guidance. The player meets emissaries of [[The Vatican]] who act as allies, leading the player through the hazards and demonic resistance.
        

## The Turning Point (Rings 6-7)

The aesthetic shifts dramatically here to a fiery, oppressive darkness.

- **Ring 6: [[地獄の六輪：Heresy]]**
    
    - The narrative bottleneck. The Vatican forces reveal their true dogma, demanding the player submit or be purged.
        
    - **The Great Schism:** The player triggers a holy war, breaking away to found [[The Vatican]].
        
    - Here, the player discovers their **[[Heretical Bloodline (異端血統)]]**, allowing them to harness the forbidden magic [[The Vatican]] fears.
        
- **Ring 7: Violence**
    
    - The war is in full swing. Vatican paladins and inquisition forces start to hunt you. However in their mind you are just in the way. They're reason for killing [[Satan]] is different than yours. 

## The Deep Hell (Rings 8-9)

The final descent. The fire gives way to biting cold and structural decay.

- **Ring 8: Fraud**
    - Deception and treacherous architecture. The remnants of fallen angels and ancient deceivers.
	* Where you meet **The [[空観協会 Vacant Order]]**
- **Ring 9: Treachery**
    
    - A frozen, crystalline wasteland. The absolute bottom of the abyss.
        
    - The final confrontation with [[Satan]], locked in the ice, and the ultimate showdown with the [[Shotgun Monk (Final Boss)]], who has by now ascended to Cardinal status to stop you.
