## Historical Context & Origins

The ArchMage μεγαλόσχημος μοναχός, holds a seat as the Chief of Education at the **Byzantine Round Table**—a secret, esoteric order of magic-wielders who protected the Emperor in Constantinople. Much like Merlin guided Arthur, Darzog guided the Eastern Empire from the shadows.

> When the historical Great Schism occurred in **1054**, it wasn't just a political disagreement over church doctrine. In the lore of _異端血統_, the Pope’s legates arrived in Constantinople not only to excommunicate the Patriarch, but to secretly purge the Byzantine Round Table. The Vatican—already operating as the hyper-violent, anti-magic zealots of the XIIIth Crusade—slaughtered Darzog’s order. Darzog was banished directly to the Heresy ring of Hell, forced to watch his legacy burn.

- Heavy, ornate gold-and-purple lamellar armor layered under ragged, ash-stained silk robes. His eyes glow with a harsh, unholy arcane light, and his body is etched with glowing, Orthodox-style iconographic tattoos.

- **The Attitude:** Cold, calculating, and dripping with centuries of patient hatred. He has none of the Vatican's screaming fanaticism; he is the quiet, terrifying calm of a master strategist. He speaks with ancient authority, treating the player less like a friend and more like a comrade. 
## Role in the Narrative (The Catalyst)

The ArchMage of the East has spent a millennium trapped in **Ring 6: [[地獄の六輪：Heresy]]**, studying the structural weaknesses of Hell and the Vatican's supply lines. He cannot wage war alone.

When the player descends into **Ring 6: [[地獄の六輪：Heresy]]**, accompanied by Vatican forces, Darzog recognizes the [[Heretical Bloodline (異端血統)]] radiating from the player—a dormant magical resonance that hasn't existed since the days of the Byzantine Round Table.

Darzog forces the confrontation. He offers the player a choice: remain a dog on [[The Vatican]]'s leash and eventually be purged, or awaken their bloodline, learn the ancient arts of the East, and found a new [[The Orthodox Church (東方正教会)]] to tear Hell apart.

## Gameplay Mechanics (The Merlin Figure)

- **The Magic System:** Interacting with Darzog through the Dialogue Manager unlocks the player's spellcasting abilities. He is the one who teaches the player how to convert demon blood and soul energy into destructive magic.
    
- **Upgrades:** Throughout Rings 6-9, Darzog acts as the player's primary hub. Finding ancient Byzantine relics or heretical texts hidden in the levels allows Darzog to upgrade the player's spells, turning them from a gun-toting mercenary into a dual-wielding engine of magical and ballistic destruction.
