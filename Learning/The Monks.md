### [[Generic Healing Monk]] 

- **The Archetype:** The quiet, gentle face of a brutal machine.
- **Role:** They patch up the player during the chaotic firefights of the early rings. 
- **The Tragedy:** He represents the rank-and-file believers who are blind to the psychotic nature of their leaders. During the Great Schism in Ring 6: [[地獄の六輪：Heresy]], the player is forced into a confrontation where a monk begs you to repent.
