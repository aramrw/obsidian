#異端血統 

> 	This circle which is reserved for those who are violent in life was separated into 3 parts: Violence against others, Violence against self and Violence against God.

![[Pasted image 20260505032825.png|322]]

> It is the punishment of those damned souls who participated in or acted on others with violence. The Rings, as they are called – are ''dedicated'' to violence towards one's neighbors, violence toward oneself and violence towards God and Nature. Some punishments include being boiled in the blood of those they persecuted, being torn to pieces by Harpies (Winged Demons with sharp claws) to being left to burn and boil in The Lake of Fire. Any attempted escapes are met by Legions of Demons, who hack at The Souls with Swords and Spears.
