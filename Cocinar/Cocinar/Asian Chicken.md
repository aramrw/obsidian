
## ⏱️ Phase 1: Prep & Marinade (5 Minutes)

1. Cut the chicken: Slice your chicken breast into uniform, bite-sized cubes.
2. Season it: Place the chicken cubes in a bowl. Toss them with a drizzle of oil, a heavy hand of Dan-O’s Original and Spicy, and a heavy pinch of cumin and paprika (plus a tiny pinch of cinnamon if you have it). Mix until completely coated.
3. Mix the liquid bomb: In a small cup, mix 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce with 1/2 teaspoon of your Thai Kitchen fish sauce. Set it aside.
4. Chop the veggies: Slice your onion and chop your chives. Leave the baby tomatoes whole.

## 🍚 Phase 2: The Rice Upgrade (3 Minutes)

1. Heat the rice: Microwave or heat up your ordered white rice until it is steaming hot.
2. Elevate it: Immediately drop 1 tablespoon of butter and a heavy shake of black pepper (or some extra chives) directly into the hot rice. Fluff it with a fork until the butter melts completely and coats the grains. Cover it so it stays warm.

## 🍳 Phase 3: The Skillet Execution (8-10 Minutes)

1. Sauté the onions: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat with a splash of oil. Toss in the onions and cook for 2 minutes until they start to soften.
2. Sear the chicken: Crank the heat up a bit and drop the seasoned chicken cubes into the pan. Spread them out. Do not touch them for 2-3 minutes so they get that beautiful, deep shawarma-style crust. Flip and cook for 2 more minutes.
3. Drop the tomatoes: Toss the whole baby tomatoes into the pan with the chicken and onions. Cook for 2 minutes until the tomato skins start to blister and wrinkle.
4. The Sauce Drop: Pour your Worcestershire and fish sauce blend directly over everything. Shake the pan instantly. The sauce will bubble violently and glaze the chicken while helping the tomatoes burst open into a rich pan juice. Cook for 1 more minute, then kill the heat.

## 🍽️ Phase 4: Plating the Masterpiece

1. The Foundation: Lay down a heavy bed of your elevated buttered rice in a wide bowl.
2. The Hot Side: Spoon the seared Dan-O's chicken, onions, and blistered tomatoes over one half of the rice.
3. The Liquid Gold: Pour every single drop of that caramelized pan sauce directly over the chicken and rice.
4. The Cold Side: Scoop a massive, cold dollop of your cucumber yogurt salad right next to the chicken.
5. The Finish: Shower the entire bowl with your fresh chopped chives.
